Before You Book

What We Can and Cannot Take

We handle most household junk, cleanout debris, furniture, appliances, and yard debris. Some materials need special disposal and may not be accepted.

Items That May Need Special Handling

Refrigerators, freezers, air conditioners, paint, tires, electronics, and certain appliances may require extra disposal steps or fees.

Items We Do Not Take

  • Hazardous chemicals
  • Fuel, oil, or wet paint
  • Asbestos-containing materials
  • Medical waste
  • Explosives or ammunition
  • Anything unsafe or illegal to haul
