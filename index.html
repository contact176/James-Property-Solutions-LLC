FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about service area, pricing, scheduling, and how to send project details.

What areas do you serve?

James Property Solutions LLC serves Indianapolis, IN and nearby areas. Send the property address and job details first so availability can be confirmed before a time slot is promised.

How quickly can I get a quote?

Many smaller jobs can be reviewed quickly from photos and a short description. Larger or more complex work may require more details before pricing is finalized.

Do you offer same-day service?

Sometimes, depending on the schedule, location, and job size. Same-week service may be available for some requests.

How is pricing determined?

Smaller jobs may have clear starting prices. Larger jobs depend on load size, square footage, condition, access, disposal needs, and total scope.

Can I text photos of the job?

Yes. Sending photos and a short description is one of the fastest ways to review junk removal, pressure washing, and cleanup requests.

What kinds of jobs are the best fit?

Junk removal, pressure washing, move-out cleanup, rental cleanouts, and recurring trash bin sanitization are the clearest best-fit services.
