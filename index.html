Frequently Asked Questions
Do you bring your own water?
Yes — for most jobs we supply our own water. For larger house washes we may use your outdoor spigot, which is standard practice industry-wide and costs you nothing extra.
What's the minimum for junk removal?
Minimum loads (roughly one large appliance or a few bags of debris) start around $99. Text us a photo of your items at (317) 903-0991 and we'll give you a price on the spot — no home visit needed for small jobs.
How soon can you come out?
We offer same-week scheduling for most services. Call or text (317) 903-0991 and we'll find a time that works. We respect your schedule and show up when we say we will.
What if I'm not happy with the results?
Simple — we come back and fix it, free of charge. 100% satisfaction is our guarantee, not just a tagline. Patrick stands behind every job personally.
Do you work with realtors and landlords?
Absolutely — our Move-In Special bundle was designed specifically for real estate agents, property managers, and investors. We can also discuss volume pricing for rental cleanouts and flip projects.
What areas do you serve?
We cover Indianapolis and all surrounding suburbs — Carmel, Fishers, Greenwood, Avon, Westfield, Noblesville, Brownsburg, Zionsville, and more. Not sure if we reach you? Just text us.
How does the Bin Guard membership work?
You sign up once for $10/month per bin. We sync your service to run the day after your regular Indy trash pickup. No scheduling needed on your end — we handle it every month automatically.
Are you licensed and insured?
James Property Solution LLC is a registered Indiana LLC. We carry liability insurance and operate fully above board. You can verify our LLC status through the Indiana Secretary of State's INBiz portal.
